About Hackathon

Devcation Hack 'N' Solve is a high-impact national hackathon hosted at IIT Delhi, bringing together developers, designers, and innovators to solve real-world problems.

Participants will collaborate in teams, build innovative solutions, and compete for exciting prizes, while receiving mentorship and industry exposure.

The hackathon encourages creativity, scalability, and impactful problem-solving across domains.
